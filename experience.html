THE EXPERIENCE

Hobnob comes from the early 19th century. Meaning ‘give and take,’ used by two people drinking to each other's health.

Also meaning to mix socially, especially with those of higher social status.The Hobnob Fest is a design, arts and music festival that features art exhibitions and a variety of concerts from contemporary to classic music around the world. With a great variety of ritms like Jazz, Blues Samba, Rock, Classic and Folk Rock, this festival will make sure to please the most unconventional tastes. Bossa Nova and Zouk will also be part of the Fest.

THE MARKET

The Market is the perfect go-to for a shopping break. With over 20 different vendors, you’re sure to find something you like. If you get carried away, you can store all your goods in lockers located within the festival

THE ZEN

Situated amongst the trees of The Island, The ZEN hosts an exclusive party experience, featuring renowned DJs and an energetic crowd. Put on a pair of headphones and be ready to dance the day and night away in The Zen.

THE BREWERY

Brooklyn's own craft Brewery comes to The Hobnob Fest this year to present The Brewery, Hobnob's on-site craft beer bar. The Brewery proudly serves HobNob Ale, a Pale Ale specially brewed for the festival!

THE HUT

At the VIP area you will find the specialty cocktail bar, featuring whiskeys of the world and our amazing variety of our old fashion cocktails. Our star bartenders will make sure to serve you only the best!!
