July 07-09 @ Randall's Island

Design and Music Festival

Hobnob comes from the early 19th century. Meaning ‘give and take,’ used by two people drinking to each other's health.

The festival aims to please a more cultured oriented crowd that have great interests in design, art and music from around the world. It combines the classics from different countries and its focused on the mingling of artists, designers and musicians that are part of that community.For those who are eclectics readers, who watches well-written classic films, and who has a refined appreciation for art. Those educated about the world and its languages, to understand world politics, and to be well-read in world history.
